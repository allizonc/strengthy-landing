Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Strengthy ("we," "us," or "our"), a workout planning and recording application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services. By using Strengthy, you agree to this Privacy Policy, which outlines our practices regarding the handling of your personal workout data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and user name when you create an account.
  • Workout Data: Information related to the workouts you track within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Workout Tracking: To provide comprehensive workout tracking, categorization, and completion analytics based on your workout patterns.
  • Analytics & Insights: To generate workout trends and insights.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app functionality, user interface, and workout planning and tracking features.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Cloud Storage: We store your financial data securely on Supabase, which maintains industry-standard security and privacy practices.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

We do not sell your personal workout information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data Security: We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your workout information.
  • Data Retention: We retain your financial information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data through the in-app "Delete Account" feature.
  • International Transfer: Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your residence, where data protection laws may differ.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal information through your account
  • Correction of inaccurate information within the app
  • Request the deletion of your personal information

To exercise these rights or if you have questions about your data, please contact us at alisonchoi@ymail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Strengthy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information from our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of Strengthy after such modifications constitutes your acknowledgment of the modified Privacy Policy.
